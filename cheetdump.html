IN FORUMS

Is Cheet for You?

Yes, Cheet is for everyone, though it has features that implies it being basically a LMS for institutions, at its core, its built for instructors and learners who have a passion for knowledge

Examination Help

Cheet provides a platform for students to effectively prepare for their course examinations by offering them, where possible, access to past questions for and their widely accepted solutions as well

Project Materials and Help

We provide access to course and projects study materials as well as "Project Mercenary" services for individuals that need that "helping hand"

Information Nexus

The cheet platform allows any and every registered school post up important information about their school and associated information that could be useful to their students and the general public affected.

Local Book Stores Access

Cheet provides a platfrom for bookstores to sell their content online and reach a wide and target range of users, students in turn have a point to compare prices and get the best deals on their study texts

Indepth Course Contents

Our platform allows a flexible amount of content be added to courses to ensure the best experience obtainable is given to our users, from class notes, quizzes and tests to various multimedia content
